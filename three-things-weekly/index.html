Three Things Weekly

Every week I pick three things that have crossed my path, send them to you and explain why I find them interesting. They could be links to articles, tweets or videos. Beers or coffees. Bars or restaurants I visit. Or they might just be things I learn along the way.

I can't promise you they will be new to you, or that they will change your life. Just that they will be things. And there will be three of them. And they will come every week.
