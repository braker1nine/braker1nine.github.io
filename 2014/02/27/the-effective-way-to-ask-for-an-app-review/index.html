The Effective Way to Ask for an App Review

Feb 27, 2014

Supertop, makers of Castro, have been experimenting with prompting users to review the app in the release notes. And it’s worked.

I have to admit I’m surprised by this. I’d expect few people see the release notes because of auto updating, and even fewer read them. Maybe it’s more than I expect or maybe a high percentage of those who read them appreciate that prompt and leave a review.
