OneNote for Mac

I would have been so excited about this 4 years ago. Now it’s just too little too late. I always preferred the OneNote editor to the Evernote version, but I’m way too invested in Evernote to look back now. It’s where I save everything I want to be able to search for later. I stopped wasting my time labeling emails and being tied to Gmail when I realized I could just save anything I wanted to keep to Evernote and have offline access to it whenever I needed.
