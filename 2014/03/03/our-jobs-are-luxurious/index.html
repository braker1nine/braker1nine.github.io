We Have Luxurious Jobs

Mar 03, 2014

Gediminas Rapolavicius:

Posts about annoying, spammy recruiters and just not feeling like working, when working from home, are popular and regular on HackerNews, /r/programming. And I won’t deny, I do often agree on those topics.

The other day, I realised how messed up that is.

Sometimes I forget how lucky I am to be in the profession I’m in with the skills I have.
