The Armory of Thought

Mar 16, 2015

J.D. Bentley:

It isn’t that the man who thinks positively attracts positive circumstances. It’s that a man who thinks positively sees the positive in all circumstances. Whatever circumstances we are faced with, we have the choice between approaching them from a place of blessedness or a place of misery, to view them as an opportunity for enlightenment and growth or as oppression.

Great piece on the value of where you place your thinking. It’s difficult to have right action without first having your thoughts focused on what is right.
