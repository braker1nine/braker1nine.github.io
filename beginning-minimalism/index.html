Beginning Minimalism➞

December 5th, 2014

Dave Paola:

Once you begin the process of slowly shedding unnecessary possessions, it becomes intoxicating. Your living space slowly becomes simpler and less cluttered, which has the unexpected effect of de-cluttering your head. It becomes a lifestyle.

I’ve been feeling this sort of tugging at my soul for the past couple of months. I have too much stuff. Too much clutter. And that stuff tends to begat more stuff. I need to replace that stuff. Upgrade it.

I’ve been trying to evaluate the things in our home. To look for things that I don’t love to look at or need. I’m going to try to share some of my struggle as I attempt to reduce my stuff.
