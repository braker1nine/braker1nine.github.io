Outshape

April 9th, 2015

For those of you who don’t know, I’ve been spending most of my time since I left ESPN working on a game called Outshape. I’m getting super close to being ready to submit to the App Store. I’m just trying to iterate the design and interaction to as close to perfection as I can get it, while I smooth out some functional edges. It’s the most fun I’ve had in years. And I’m delighted to be able to work hard without having to work all night.

I’ve started a blog where I’m going to document the development process of Outshape. From the inspiration, to early mocks, to the stresses of sharing a thing I’m making with the world. I’m sure some of that will come over here, but I’m going to post anything Outshape specific over there.

So go sign up for the email list if you want to get the details on how Outshape came to be!
